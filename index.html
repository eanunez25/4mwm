Strategy

4M Wealth Management seeks positive abolsute returns by exploiting pricing inefficiencies in equities driven by various events such as market downturns, company crisises, mergers, or new executive management. These events create opportunies for higher returns with relatively lower risk. If no opportunities are present, the fund may hold a large cash position and utilize derivates trading to artificially create advantageous purchases and sales.

Non-Diversified

The depth of fundamental analysis allows the fund to have a non-diversified focus. A majority of capital will be invested in no more that fifteen equity positions.

Deep Value Investing

The fund aims to purchase investments at discounts over half of its intrinsic value. Our valuation methodology is based off the expectation of long-term capital gains. Exploiting market events have allowed for deep discounts.

Macro-Technical

The fund will seek gains in excess of the market and observe technical trends that indincate a major market direction shift. The fund will shift asset allocation from overweight equities to cash for capital preservation.

Derivatives Trading

The fund itilizes options contracts to generate cash flow and create advantegous entry and exit positions for equity invesmtents.
